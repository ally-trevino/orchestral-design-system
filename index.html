Introduction to the Happening Brand

Happening is an event-production company that puts together concerts, parties, shows, and more. They are focused on promoting both their events and their services as second-to-none for a wide variety of brands and clients. Their image is energetic, youthful, and fun.

About Orchestral

As a brand, Happening seeks to to arrange a variety of concerts, parties, and shows through clever and thorough planning. Just as the many instruments of an orchestra come together to produce beautiful and complex compositions, the design elements of Happening must come together to promote events and deliver services harmoniously.
